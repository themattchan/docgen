Full description

One bedroom apartment situated on the fourth floor of this popular development of Devons Mansions, conveniently located to a number of local amenities. This property comprises a large double bedroom, good sized living room with wooden flooring, three piece bathroom suite and a fully fitted kitchen. This property benefits from private off street parking and an abundance of natural light.
